Aerialist · Distinguished Circus entertainment
Cassandra first appeared on stage in the womb. The stage tracked her through youth as a dancer and deposited her in San Francisco to train with the circus. She now performs and makes art on rope and aerial fabric, and specializes in wielding her striking height at high velocity. Her imagination thrives between 20 and 30 feet above ground, where she grapples with human limitations and seeks glimpses of the sublime.

Thinking of having an aerialist at your event? You're brilliant. Here are a few pointers on how to make it happen.

  • What are you looking for? Cassandra can provide a feature act to a single piece of music, or ambient performance that sets a mood and lasts longer. For an evening of entertainment, Cassandra can also organize multiple performers.
  • Aerial acts require a safe rigging point. This requires access to the ceiling, an exposed structural beam (or professionally installed rigging point), and a minimum ceiling height of 18' for tissu or 20' for rope. For outdoor performances, please contact Cassandra for more information.
