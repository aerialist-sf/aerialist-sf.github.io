Thinking of having an aerialist at your event? Here are a few pointers.

  • Cassandra can provide a feature act to a single piece of music, or ambient performance that sets a mood and lasts longer. For an evening of entertainment, Cassandra can also organize multiple performers.
  • Aerial acts require a safe rigging point. This means access to the ceiling, an exposed structural beam (or professionally installed rigging point), and a minimum ceiling height of 18' for tissu or 20' for rope. For outdoor performances, please contact for more information.
